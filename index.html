Our Story

About Us.

A legacy of craftsmanship, scale, and integrity — woven into every garment we produce for India's leading retail chains.

Our Journey

From Factory Floor to Retail Shelf.

Varun Fabrica LLP was founded with a single conviction — that Indian apparel manufacturing could meet the quality and scale demands of modern retail chains, without compromise.

Starting with a focused production unit in Kolkata, the company systematically built up its manufacturing capabilities, quality systems, and retail partnerships across India's leading value and mass-market chains.

Today, Varun Fabrica operates as part of a vertically integrated group — spanning fabric sourcing and development (Varun Creation), specialised shirts manufacturing (Tanvi Apparel), and the flagship manufacturing entity, delivering 6 lakh+ pieces monthly.

01

Foundation

Varun Fabrica LLP Established

Company incorporated with a focus on scalable apparel manufacturing for India's organised retail sector. GST: 19ABBFV7595D1ZP

02

Infrastructure

EIGMEF Apparel Park, Kolkata

Moved into the EIGMEF Apparel Park with dedicated units A3/1, A3/2, A3/3 — enabling multi-line production at full capacity.

03

Retail Expansion

6+ Major Retail Partnerships

Established supply relationships with Baazar Kolkata, Vishal Mega Mart, V2 Retail, V-Mart, Style Baazar and M-Baazar.

04

Today

6 Lakh+ Pieces Monthly

Operating at full industrial scale with own brands HOMBREVO, RIVYA, and NINOS alongside B2B manufacturing for India's top retailers.
